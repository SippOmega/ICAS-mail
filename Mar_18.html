1. Monday:
Anime Screening in SAF G34 @ 18:30

Ambassador's choice

Our ambassador Mob is hosting this week, and will just show some things he feels like, no real theme here. 

The first of the two he intends to show is Jujutsu Kaisen, which needs no introduction.

The second is Tenkuu Danzai Skelter+Heaven. When a mysterious entity suddenly appears in the center of Tokyo, the Alta Mira Agency is tasked to repel the extraterrestrial threat. Otsuya Funagai must guide his all-female unit of "Battle Sole" pilots to take down the strange being no matter the cost. However, his intimate relationship with one of the pilots, Rin Ichikawa, may spell trouble for the team and the mission itself.
