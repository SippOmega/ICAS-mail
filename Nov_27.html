1. Monday:
Anime Screening in SAF G34 @ 18:30

Theme: CGDCT

CGDCT stands for Cute Girls Doing Cute Things, and this really is the entirety of the genre. If you're looking for some light-hearted comedy and relaxing vibes, this is the session for you.

First on the list is Yama no Susume. After a playground accident caused her to become afraid of heights, Aoi Yukimura, gave up on her passion for climbing and turned her focus to indoor hobbies. So absorbed in these hobbies, Aoi grows up to be a shy and timid high schooler, but after reuniting with an old climbing friend and awakening old memories, Aoi starts to climb again and slowly break out of her shell.  

Next is Non Non Biyori, about Hotaru Ichijou who moves from Tokyo to an isolated village, joining a school with only five students. Despite the invconveniences of village life, Hotaru falls in love with the charms of countryside life and develops a strong bond with the 4 other students, bringing them knowledge of the big city and a new outlook on life.

Last but not least is Yuru Camp. Rin Shima's idea of a perfect holiday is camping alone near Mount Fuji, enjoying the solitary life. However, when a lost Nadeshiko Kagamihara winds up at her campsite, the two form a connection, leading them to consider the idea of more camping together.

3. Friday:
Movie Screening in Huxley 213 (Clore) @ 18:30

This week we are watching one of the most critically acclaimed anime films of all time, Spirited Away, a Studio Ghibli classic.

Stubborn, spoiled, and naïve, 10-year-old Chihiro Ogino is less than pleased when she and her parents discover an abandoned amusement park on the way to their new house. Cautiously venturing inside, she realizes that there is more to this place than meets the eye, as strange things begin to happen once dusk falls. Ghostly apparitions and food that turns her parents into pigs are just the start—Chihiro has unwittingly crossed over into the spirit world. Now trapped, she must summon the courage to live and work amongst spirits, with the help of the enigmatic Haku and the cast of unique characters she meets along the way.
